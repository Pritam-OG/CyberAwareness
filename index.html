NO MORE ONLINE FRAUDS

Cyber scams are fraudulent activities conducted through digital platforms to deceive individuals or businesses into providing sensitive information, financial assets, or personal data. These scams exploit technology, psychology, and social engineering to manipulate victims. Common types of cyber scams include phishing, ransomware attacks, identity theft, online shopping fraud, and social media scams.

2. He has to deposit Rs.8,200 in a STATE BANK OF INDIA Account Number and come for the interview with the pay slip and also that it was said in the email that this amount is refundable.

3. The Emails traced were from all foreign countries , and the BANK Account were also fake to which the money was deposited and the amount was immediately withdrawal from ATMs
